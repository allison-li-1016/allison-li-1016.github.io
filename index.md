## About Me

I am a **3rd year PhD student at the Univeristy of Washington**, advised by [Dr. Neda Bagheri](https://bagherilab.com/). I am broadly interested in building spatiotemporal models for designing living therapeutics, and using machine learning models to identify disease signals.

Before this, I also completed my BS in both computer science and biochemistry at the University of Washington. I previously worked at the Fred Hutch Cancer Center with [Dr. Trevor Bedford](https://bedford.io/team/) on phylogenetic modeling of Norovirus, and at UW BioFAB (now the Institute of Protein Design) under [Dr. Eric Klavins](https://klavinslab.org/) designing reproducible workflows and inventory tracking for automating benchwork protocols.
